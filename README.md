## Frequently Asked Questions

**Who is RxCalcPro designed for?**
RxCalcPro is designed for licensed healthcare professionals 
including pharmacists, nurses, physicians, and pharmacy 
technicians.

**Does the app work offline?**
Yes. All calculators work fully offline. 
No internet connection is required.

**What is included in the free version?**
- Dilution Calculator
- Alligation Calculator
- IV Flow Rate Calculator
- BMI Calculator
- BSA Calculator (Mosteller)
- Creatinine Clearance (Cockcroft-Gault)

**What is included in the $1.99 unlock?**
- BUD Reference (USP 795/797)
- Unit Converter
- Pediatric Dosing Calculator
- Ideal Body Weight (Devine)
- Corrected Calcium
- Anion Gap + Delta-Delta Ratio

One-time purchase. No subscription. Unlocks forever.

**I purchased but lost access. How do I restore?**
Open RxCalcPro, tap any locked calculator,
then tap "Restore Purchase" on the purchase screen.

**How do I request a refund?**
Refund requests are handled by Apple directly.
Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in 
with your Apple ID.
